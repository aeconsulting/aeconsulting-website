History

Founded in 2009 AE Consulting creates a great performance history and has successfully implemented number of projects in Armenia and worldwide. During its work AEC has generated a network of great partners around the world, that include major players from private sector as well as financial funding organizations. Back in 2009 AEC has been the first to talk about the lack of attention to the energy efficiency. With small but firm steps and actions AEC has created an environment that boosts development of EE in Armenia. AEC success is firmly based on the knowledge and service gained with hard work and self education. AEC has successfully worked with number of local financial institutions as well as with international ones, accumulating knowledge that is used to create sustainable solutions for our clients.Over more than 10 years AEC has accumulated knowledge that is now sharing with educational systems.
